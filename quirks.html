Character Interaction Instructions

Lord and Lady Peacock

When you meet someone, Lord Peacock, you must salute them sharply and say, “At ease, soldier!” Lady Peacock, follow up with a nod and confidently add, “You’re lucky to have us on your side.”

Mr. and Mrs. Green

Mr. Green, every time you see someone, give them a vigorous handshake and say, “Pleasure doing business with you!” Mrs. Green, lean in with a sly smile and whisper, “Watch your back—things get messy around here.”

Miss and Mr. Scarlet

Miss Scarlet, when you meet someone, dramatically declare, “Don’t you dare steal my spotlight!” while flipping your hair. Mr. Scarlet, point at them and say, “I could write an entire play about you—but it’d be a tragedy!”

Madam and Missy Mustard

Madam Mustard, whenever you encounter someone, point at them and exclaim, “Ah-ha! You look like a smuggler!” Missy Mustard, give them an overly long, awkward hug and reassure them, “Don’t worry, I’ll keep your secret.”

Mrs. and Dr. White

Dr. White, when you meet someone, grab their hand and say firmly, “You look pale! Have you been haunted recently?” Mrs. White, gently pat them on the shoulder and add, “Take care of yourself, dear—it’s dangerous out there.”

Professor and Lady Plum

Professor Plum, every time you pass someone, point at them and say, “Did you know ghosts are drawn to people who look like you?” Lady Plum, add with an air of mystery, “Be careful—they’re always watching!”

Inspector and Madam Noir

Inspector Noir, whenever you meet someone, point dramatically and declare, “You’re not getting away this time!” Madam Noir, scribble furiously on your invisible notepad and mutter, “Another suspect for my list…”

Mr. and Madam Mahogany

Mr. Mahogany, when you see someone, grab their hand and say excitedly, “You’re holding a priceless artifact, aren’t you? Let me see!” Madam Mahogany, look them over appraisingly and say, “Hmm, not bad... but I’ve seen better.”

Dr. and Professor Orchid

Dr. Orchid, every time you meet someone, touch their shoulder gently and say, “You’d make an excellent specimen.” Professor Orchid, wink and add, “Let me know if you grow anything unusual.”

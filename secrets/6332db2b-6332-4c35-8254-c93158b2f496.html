Encountering Ghosts

When you see a ghost, snap to attention immediately!

Lord Peacock, you must take command with a crisp salute and demand a full debriefing about the ghost’s demise. Address them as “General” or “Colonel” and ask them pointed, military-style questions about their situation.

Lady Peacock, your role is to take meticulous imaginary notes and nod solemnly as if their answers are critical intelligence.

Keep the performance going until the ghost departs.

When you Encounter Other Guests

Lord Peacock, you must salute them sharply and say, “At ease, soldier!”

Lady Peacock, follow up with a nod and confidently add, “You’re lucky to have us on your side.”

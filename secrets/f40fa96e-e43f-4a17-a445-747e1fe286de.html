TOP SECRET - MOTIVE!!!

Dr. and Professor Orchid:

"It was my fault. One of my experiments had unintended consequences, and a guest paid the price. The Hines couple started investigating, getting too close to the truth. Professor Orchid and I knew what would happen if they exposed us. We couldn’t let that happen, so we took steps to protect ourselves. It was the only way."

Encountering Ghosts

When you see a ghost, treat it like a groundbreaking discovery!

Dr. Orchid, attempt to “capture” the ghosts in invisible containers, frantically muttering about fixing your mistake.

Professor Orchid, make notes about their chemical or botanical traits, as if they’re part of your next research paper.

Keep your scientific investigation going until the ghosts leave.

When you Encounter Other Guests

Dr. Orchid, every time you meet someone, touch their shoulder gently and say, “You’d make an excellent specimen.”

Professor Orchid, wink and add, “Let me know if you grow anything unusual.”

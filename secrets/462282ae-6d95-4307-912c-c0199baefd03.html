TOP SECRET - MOTIVE!!!

Miss and Mr. Scarlet:

"The Hines couple humiliated me in public, criticizing my work and tarnishing my reputation. They had no idea how much that hurt, how much it threatened my career. Mr. Scarlet saw my pain and, inspired by one of his stories, suggested a way to stop them from ever speaking ill of me again. We knew it was risky, but the idea of letting them ruin me was unbearable."

Encountering Ghosts

When you encounter a ghost, it’s showtime!

Miss Scarlet, you must dramatically deliver tragic monologues or heartfelt poetry about the ghosts’ “tragic love story.”

Mr. Scarlet, you’ll need to match her energy, declaring your sorrow or theorizing about the betrayal and doom they’ve suffered.

Keep your performance as over-the-top as possible until the ghost is gone.

When you Encounter Other Guests

Miss Scarlet, when you meet someone, dramatically declare, “Don’t you dare steal my spotlight!” while flipping your hair.

Mr. Scarlet, point at them and say, “I could write an entire play about you—but it’d be a tragedy!”

TOP SECRET - MOTIVE!!!

Mr. and Madam Mahogany:

"They called me a fraud. The Hines couple discovered that some of the artifacts I sold them weren’t authentic, and they demanded restitution. Madam Mahogany reminded me that we couldn’t let them ruin us—our reputation, our livelihood. It wasn’t easy, but we did what we had to do to keep our secrets safe."

Encountering Ghosts

When a ghost enters, treat it like a rare artifact!

Mr. Mahogany, begin appraising the ghost’s “value,” pointing out unique features and discussing how much they’d fetch at auction.

Madam Mahogany, assist by trying to “sell” the ghost to others in the room, hyping their rarity and importance.

Keep your antiques show alive until the ghost disappears.

When you Encounter Other Guests

Mr. Mahogany, when you see someone, grab their hand and say excitedly, “You’re holding a priceless artifact, aren’t you? Let me see!”

Madam Mahogany, look them over appraisingly and say, “Hmm, not bad... but I’ve seen better.”

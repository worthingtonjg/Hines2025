Backstory: Mrs. Peacock’s penchant for gossip means she knows everyone’s darkest secrets, and Lord Peacock’s military record has a classified section he refuses to discuss.

Mrs. and Dr. White

Backstory: Mrs. White was seen arguing with the victim over unpaid wages, and Dr. White’s medical practice lost its license after a patient’s untimely death.

Dr. and Professor Orchid

Backstory: Dr. Orchid’s experiments in exotic poisons were banned after a deadly accident, and Professor Orchid’s research grants have suspiciously disappeared.

Professor and Lady Plum

Inspector and Madam Noir

Mr. and Madam Mahogany

Mr. Mahogany wears a brown leather jacket, beige scarf, and a satchel filled with “artifacts.”

Madam Mahogany wears a chocolate-brown dress with gold accents and vintage jewelry.

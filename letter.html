Subject: Your Invitation to the Hines Mansion

From: Inspector Noir

Date: January 10, 2025

Dear Guests,

You have been summoned to the Hines Mansion on January 20th for reasons that go beyond mere social pleasantries. As you may know, Nathan and Brin Hines, the heirs to the Hines family fortune, were found tragically deceased in the mansion's parlor. Their deaths were untimely, suspicious, and shrouded in mystery.

Each of you has been linked to the Hines in one way or another—through business, friendship, or perhaps deeper, darker connections. This gathering is no coincidence. Your presence is required because you are among the key suspects in unraveling this enigma.

As we dine and deliberate, it is my duty to uncover the truth. Know this: no one leaves until the mystery is solved.

I look forward to your cooperation.

Sincerely,

Inspector Noir

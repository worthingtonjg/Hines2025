Lodge ~ Guest Log

May 18, 2019

"As soon as I walked in, I felt a heaviness in the air. It wasn’t until I tried to sleep that the true strangeness began. The sound of a typewriter—faint but unmistakable—filled the room. At first, I thought someone was playing a prank, but there was no one else staying near me. I also heard what sounded like cards shuffling, but when I got up to check, nothing was there. I left the lights on for the rest of the night."

James Conway, Las Vegas

October 15, 2020

"The Lodge felt cozy at first, but by nightfall, it was anything but. I woke up to the sound of footsteps pacing above me, though I was on the top floor. At one point, I thought I heard someone whisper my name, but when I turned on the light, the room was empty."

Hannah Black, Seattle

March 5, 2022

"This room has a strange energy. Around 3 AM, the lights flickered, and the sound of shuffling cards seemed to come from the desk. When I checked, nothing was there. I couldn’t shake the feeling that I was being watched."

Michael Ray, San Diego

January 2, 2023

"The Lodge’s history fascinated me, but staying here was unsettling. I heard faint laughter echoing in the room, and when I looked in the mirror, I thought I saw a figure behind me. The next morning, my suitcase was on the opposite side of the room from where I’d left it."

Olivia Green, Chicago

July 19, 2024

"Nathan always found solace in this room, but I felt its darkness. He thought the history of the lodge was a source of strength, but I fear it held secrets we weren’t meant to uncover. Someone used that history to twist fate against us."

Bryn Hines

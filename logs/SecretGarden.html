Secret Garden ~ Guest Log

April 14, 2018

"I had heard about the ghostly gardener, but I never thought I’d experience it myself. Around 2 AM, I heard the faint sound of footsteps on the wooden floor and the rustling of leaves. When I looked into the mirror, I saw what appeared to be a man tending to invisible plants. The most unsettling part was that he seemed so real."

Angela Pearson, Portland

March 19, 2013

"The Secret Garden lived up to its name—I felt like I wasn’t alone the entire time. I woke up to the sound of faint crying, and the mirror showed a faint, misty outline of a person behind me. When I turned around, no one was there."

Eleanor James, Chicago

July 25, 2015

"I heard the soft sound of digging outside my window. When I went to look, there was nothing there but freshly disturbed dirt near the flowerbed. I felt an overwhelming sadness while staying in this room. It’s beautiful but haunting."

Greg Turner, Salt Lake City

October 31, 2019

"Halloween night in the Secret Garden—what could go wrong? Everything. I saw a faint glow coming from the corner of the room where an old flower vase was placed. It looked as if someone was holding it. I also smelled fresh roses, though there were no flowers in the room."

Hailey Rogers, Boston

June 2, 2022

"I was drawn to the Secret Garden room because of its name, but I didn’t expect it to feel so alive. Late at night, I heard whispers and the sound of leaves rustling. When I woke up, I found a single white rose lying on the nightstand. I still can’t explain where it came from."

Natalie Ortiz, Miami

July 19, 2024

"This garden held such beauty, but it also holds the weight of betrayal. Nathan whispered to me once that someone envied our happiness. Could they have planted the seeds of our downfall here? The whispering leaves seem to answer: yes."

Bryn Hines

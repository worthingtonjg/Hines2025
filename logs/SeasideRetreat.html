Seaside Retreat ~ Guest Log

August 9, 2021

"This was my first stay at the Hines Mansion, and it will be my last. I could hear the faint crash of waves and the toll of a ship’s bell—it was so vivid, I almost believed the ocean was outside. Around dawn, I felt a presence behind me, as if someone was leaning over the bed. It wasn’t threatening, but it was unnerving. When I got up, I found a single seashell on the windowsill. I’m not sure how it got there—but I didn’t put it there."

Kevin Morris, Chicago

July 4, 2016

"I stayed here over the holiday, and it was an unforgettable experience. I heard the sound of waves lapping against the shore and what sounded like distant voices calling out. At one point, I thought I felt someone sit at the edge of the bed, but no one was there."

Hannah West, Los Angeles

December 11, 2018

"The Seaside Retreat felt oddly peaceful during the day, but at night, the sound of a ship’s bell echoed faintly. I dreamed of being on a ship in a storm, and when I woke up, my pillow was damp as if I’d been splashed with water. It’s a memory I’ll never forget."

Matt Parker, Seattle

March 30, 2020

"I’d heard about the ghost in this room and didn’t believe it until I experienced it myself. Around midnight, the sound of waves grew louder, and I smelled salt in the air. I also felt a soft breeze, though the windows were shut tight. It was surreal."

Emily Hayes, Houston

September 15, 2023

"I stayed in this room on a business trip and woke up to the sound of creaking wood, like a ship swaying on the ocean. The sound stopped as soon as I turned on the light, but it was so vivid I couldn’t shake the feeling I wasn’t alone."

Adam Reed, New York

July 19, 2024

"The waves seem to echo the storm in my mind. Nathan once confided in me about a mysterious letter he received—its warnings were dire, but he dismissed it. Now, I wonder if the sender was closer than we thought, someone who wanted us out of the picture. Why didn’t we see it coming?"

Bryn Hines

Victorian Rose ~ Guest Log

June 3, 2012

"The room was cold—unbearably cold for June. I thought it was the air conditioning, but when I checked, it was turned off. In the middle of the night, I woke up to find my blankets pulled off the bed. At first, I thought it was my imagination, but then I saw it: a faint shadow on the wall that didn’t match anything in the room. It lingered for a moment before vanishing. I didn’t sleep much after that."

Richard Bell, Denver

November 9, 2010

"I felt uneasy the moment I stepped into the room. Around midnight, I woke up to hear faint whispers, though I couldn’t make out the words. My partner said they felt something cold brush against their arm. The next morning, we found scratches on the wooden bed frame that hadn’t been there before."

Allison Rhodes, Las Vegas

January 23, 2015

"I was staying here for work and thought it would be peaceful. I was wrong. The air grew icy, and I saw my breath in the room. I heard what sounded like a woman crying softly. I couldn’t pinpoint the source, but it made the hairs on my neck stand up. I left early the next morning."

Thomas Grayson, San Francisco

August 14, 2017

"My daughter kept pointing to the corner of the room, saying there was a lady there. I couldn’t see anything, but she insisted someone was standing there. Later that night, I felt a sudden chill and heard a faint laugh, though we were alone in the room."

Melissa Carter, Phoenix

May 8, 2021

"I stayed in this room during a family trip. Around 3 AM, I saw what looked like a shadow moving along the wall. It felt like someone was watching me. I tried to dismiss it, but the uneasy feeling stayed with me the entire night."

Jason Webb, Austin

July 19, 2024

"This room holds secrets Nathan dared not share with me while we lived. The cold seems to echo the chill I felt when I learned of the business deals he was forced into—deals with dangerous men who had too much to lose if the truth came out. Could they have followed us even here?"

Bryn Hines

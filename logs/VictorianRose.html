Victorian Rose ~ Guest Log

June 3, 2012

"The room was cold—unbearably cold for June. I thought it was the air conditioning, but when I checked, it was turned off. In the middle of the night, I woke up to find my blankets pulled off the bed. At first, I thought it was my imagination, but then I saw it: a faint shadow on the wall that didn’t match anything in the room. It lingered for a moment before vanishing. I didn’t sleep much after that."

Richard Bell, Denver

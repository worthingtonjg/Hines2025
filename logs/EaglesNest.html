Eagles Nest ~ Guest Log

March 12, 2016

"The views were breathtaking during the day, but at night, the room felt different. Around 2 AM, I heard soft knocking on the walls. It seemed to come from everywhere and nowhere. When I went to investigate, the sound stopped. I’m not sure if it was my imagination or something more."

Rachel Simmons, Boise

October 4, 2018

"I woke up to find my suitcase toppled over, though I’m sure I’d left it standing upright. My window rattled violently, though the night was calm. I couldn’t shake the feeling that someone—or something—was in the room with me."

Victor Caldwell, Dallas

February 28, 2021

"The wind seemed to whisper through the cracks of the windows, though they were tightly shut. At one point, I heard what sounded like footsteps above me, but I was on the top floor. I didn’t sleep much that night."

Lila Davis, Denver

July 10, 2023

"The Eagle’s Nest felt strangely alive. At 3 AM, the room creaked as though someone was walking across the floor. I heard faint whispers, like a conversation happening just out of earshot. The next morning, my phone, which had been on the nightstand, was on the floor."

Tom Harris, Chicago

July 19, 2024

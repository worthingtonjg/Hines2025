Winter Dreams ~ Guest Log

January 6, 2015

"The cold in this room was unlike anything I’d ever felt. It chilled me to the bone, even with the heater on. In the middle of the night, I thought I felt a hand on my shoulder, but when I turned around, I was alone."

Samantha Ray, Salt Lake City

December 22, 2017

"I stayed here during the holidays, and it was far from festive. Around midnight, I heard what sounded like someone crying softly. The sound seemed to come from the corner of the room, but when I checked, it stopped. I couldn’t sleep after that."

Jonathan Price, San Francisco

February 14, 2019

"Valentine’s Day in the Winter Dreams Room was memorable for all the wrong reasons. The room grew so cold that the windows frosted over. I woke up to find my glass of water frozen solid. I can’t explain it, but I’ll never forget it."

Melanie Carter, Phoenix

October 9, 2022

"The chill in the room wasn’t the worst part. I saw what looked like faint footprints in the frost on the windowpane. They seemed to be moving, as though someone was walking outside—but I was on the second floor."

Eric Daniels, Denver

July 19, 2024

"This room was always Nathan’s favorite in the winter months. He said the cold made him feel alive. But now I can’t help but think it held something darker. Someone knew how much we loved this place and used it against us. Could the winter’s chill have been their warning?"

Bryn Hines
